Holi Festival

Holi is a Hindu spring festival, also known as the festival of colors or the festival of love. It is celebrated mainly in India and Nepal, but has also spread to other parts of Asia and the Western world. The festival signifies the victory of good over evil, the arrival of spring, and the end of winter.

During Holi, people gather to smear each other with vibrant colored powders and water, play music, dance, and feast on various traditional sweets and snacks. It is a time of joy, forgiveness, and renewal of relationships.
